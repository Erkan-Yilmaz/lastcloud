lastcloud

Enter your LastFM username below and I'll try to find the top 100 artists you listen to who look like they might upload tracks to Soundcloud.

I'm not perfect, since there are some Soundcloud users who have taken musician names as their own. But you might be pleasantly surprised to find some musicians you like on Soundcloud.

This is a pure HTML and JavaScript app. You won't need to login to LastFm or Soundcloud, or bring oAuth to the dance. Code is also on Github if you have ideas for fixing/improving it.
